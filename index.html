How It Works

You and a bunch of other players drop into a procedurally generated dungeon. Loot treasure, fight monsters, backstab your rivals. Items and loot are Solana assets you actually own.

Loot Everything

Gold, gems, weapons, potions. If it fits in your bag, grab it. Bag's full? Get a bigger bag.

Fight Monsters

Skeletons, mimics, and worse. Beat them for rare drops, or just wait for someone else to do the hard work and steal their loot.

Trust No One

That chest might be a mimic. That "ally" is definitely going to stab you. Paranoia is a core feature, honestly.

On-Chain Loot

Every piece of treasure is a real Solana asset. Trade it, hoard it, flex on your friends. Your loot, your wallet.

What It Looks Like

Every run generates a fresh dungeon. Everyone's in it at the same time. Way too many goblins, never enough loot.
